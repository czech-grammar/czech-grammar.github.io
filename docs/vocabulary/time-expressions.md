## Days of the Week (Dny v týdnu)

| Czech | English | Gender |
|:------|:--------|:-------|
| **pondělí** | Monday | n |
| **úterý** | Tuesday | n |
| **středa** | Wednesday | f |
| **čtvrtek** | Thursday | m |
| **pátek** | Friday | m |
| **sobota** | Saturday | f |
| **neděle** | Sunday | f |

### Using Days

Use **v + accusative** to say "on" a day:
- *v pondělí* - on Monday
- *v úterý* - on Tuesday
- *ve středu* - on Wednesday (note the accusative change!)
- *ve čtvrtek* - on Thursday

## Months (Měsíce)

| Czech | English | Czech | English |
|:------|:--------|:------|:--------|
| **leden** | January | **červenec** | July |
| **únor** | February | **srpen** | August |
| **březen** | March | **září** | September |
| **duben** | April | **říjen** | October |
| **květen** | May | **listopad** | November |
| **červen** | June | **prosinec** | December |

### Using Months

Use **v + locative** to say "in" a month:
- *v lednu* - in January
- *v únoru* - in February
- *v březnu* - in March
- *v září* - in September (září is neuter!)

:::tip Gender Note
All months are **masculine** except **září** (September), which is **neuter**.
:::

## Telling Time (Kolik je hodin?)

### Hours

Czech uses different forms of *hodina* depending on the number:

| Time | Czech | Rule |
|:-----|:------|:-----|
| 1:00 | *jedna hodina* | singular |
| 2:00-4:00 | *dvě/tři/čtyři hodiny* | plural (2-4) |
| 5:00+ | *pět/šest... hodin* | genitive plural |

### Quarter Hours

| Time | Czech | Literal |
|:-----|:------|:--------|
| 8:15 | *čtvrt na devět* | quarter to nine |
| 8:30 | *půl deváté* | half of the ninth |
| 8:45 | *třičtvrtě na devět* | three-quarters to nine |

:::info Simple Alternative
You can also use digital-style time: *osm patnáct* (8:15), *osm třicet* (8:30).
:::

### Times of Day

| Czech | English | Usage |
|:------|:--------|:------|
| **ráno** | morning | early morning |
| **dopoledne** | late morning | before noon |
| **poledne** | noon | midday |
| **odpoledne** | afternoon | after noon |
| **večer** | evening | evening |
| **noc** | night | nighttime |

## Dates (Datum)

Dates use **ordinal numbers** in the **genitive case**:

- *15. května* = *patnáctého května* (15th of May)
- *1. ledna* = *prvního ledna* (1st of January)

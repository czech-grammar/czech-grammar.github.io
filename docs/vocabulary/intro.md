# Vocabulary (Slovní zásoba)

Build your Czech vocabulary with organized word lists by topic. Each section includes essential words with their genders and practical usage notes.

## Topics Covered

### Everyday Life
- **Family** (Rodina) - Family members and relationships
- **Body** (Tělo) - Body parts
- **Food** (Jídlo) - Common foods and drinks

### Time & Numbers
- **Numbers** (Čísla) - Cardinal and ordinal numbers
- **Days & Months** (Dny a měsíce) - Calendar vocabulary

### Places
- **City** (Město) - Places around town

### Communication
- **Common Phrases** (Běžné fráze) - Greetings and everyday expressions

## Learning Tips

1. **Learn nouns with their gender** - *muž* (m), *žena* (f), *město* (n)
2. **Practice in context** - Use words in sentences, not just isolated
3. **Group related words** - Learn *otec, matka, syn, dcera* together
4. **Notice patterns** - Many Czech words share roots with other Slavic languages

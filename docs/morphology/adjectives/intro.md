# Adjectives (Přídavná jména)

Adjectives describe nouns. In Czech, they must **agree** with the noun they modify in:
1.  **Gender**
2.  **Number**
3.  **Case**

## Position
Adjectives usually come **before** the noun.
*   ***Dobrý*** *den.* (Good day.)
*   ***Velké*** *město.* (Big city.)

If they come after the noun, it is usually for poetic emphasis or in specific scientific classifications (*kyselina sírová* - sulfuric acid).

## Types
There are two main types of adjectives based on their ending in the dictionary form (Nominative Singular Masculine):
1.  **Hard**: Ends in **-ý** (*mladý*).
2.  **Soft**: Ends in **-í** (*jarní*).

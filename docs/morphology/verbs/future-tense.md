# Future Tense (Budoucí čas)

The formation of the future tense depends entirely on the **Aspect** of the verb.

## 1. Imperfective Verbs (Process)
Use the future form of **být** + the **Infinitive**.

| Person | Být (Future) | Infinitive | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Já | **budu** | pracovat | I will be working |
| Ty | **budeš** | pracovat | You will be working |
| On | **bude** | pracovat | He will be working |
| My | **budeme** | pracovat | We will be working |
| Vy | **budete** | pracovat | You will be working |
| Oni | **budou** | pracovat | They will be working |

## 2. Perfective Verbs (Result)
Perfective verbs do not have a present tense. Their "present" conjugation actually expresses the **Future**.

*   **Infinitive**: *udělat* (to do/finish)
*   **Conjugation**:
    *   *Já udělám* = I will do (and finish).
    *   *Ty uděláš* = You will do.
    *   *On udělá* = He will do.

*Note: You CANNOT say "budu udělat". That is a serious grammar error.*

## 3. Motion Verbs
A small group of motion verbs form the future with the prefix **po-**.
*   *jít* (to go) → *půjdu* (I will go)
*   *jet* (to go by vehicle) → *pojedu* (I will go)
*   *letět* (to fly) → *poletím* (I will fly)
